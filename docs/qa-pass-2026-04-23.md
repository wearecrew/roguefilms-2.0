Automated walk of the staging build ahead of client review. Data gathered via a
dedicated Playwright spec (`code/tests/qa-pass.spec.js`) at two viewports:

- **Desktop:** Chromium, 1280×720 (Playwright default)
- **Mobile:** iPhone 14 Pro via WebKit, 393×852

Pages inspected: `/`, `/talent`, `/music-videos`, `/film-tv`,
`/directors-roster/andrew-de-zen`, `/director-showreels/welcome-to-my-bank-2`.

Raw findings + full-page screenshots are at `test-results/qa-pass/`.

Controller version on every page: **v0.13.0-phase3**. No horizontal overflow at
either viewport on any page. No console errors on desktop. Every page returned

## Things to fix before client review

### 1. Homepage work grid is 2-col on mobile — user flagged

At 393px viewport the `.home_grid` computes to `196.5px 196.5px`. The user wants
this single-column on mobile, consistent with Music Videos / Film & TV /
Director pages which already collapse correctly.

### 2. Talent page has a large empty navy block below the list on mobile — user flagged

Visually confirmed in `mobile-safari/02-talent-full.png`. The background video
doesn't fill viewport height on mobile; after the directors list the page keeps
scrolling through an empty navy slab before the footer. User's intent: video
pinned at 100vh with the directors list scrolling inside it.

### 3. Hero video audio toggle doesn't work on mobile — user flagged

`.hero_controls` renders with `href="#"` and the v0.12.0 mute-toggle-follows-cursor
pattern has no touch equivalent, so mobile users can't unmute. Agreed plan:
revert hero to a tap-to-open-lightbox on mobile (same pattern as other tiles).

### 4. Social footer icons are unlinked across every page

All 5 `.social-link` anchors point to `href="#"` on Homepage, Talent,
Music Videos, Film & TV, Director detail, Showreel detail. Need the real
Instagram / Vimeo / Facebook / X / LinkedIn URLs.

### 5. Mobile nav contact email is not a mailto

`.mobile-nav-contact` anchor on every page reads `mail@roguefilms.co.uk` but
`href="#"`. Should be `href="mailto:mail@roguefilms.co.uk"`.

### 6. Homepage news cards are unlinked

Three news articles in the News section render with `href="#"`:
- The Night Manager 2: Press
- DAVID REVIEWS PICK OF THE DAY - TINY FEET
- David Reviews Pick Of The Day - Samaritans

Either wire them to their News CMS item, or hide the News block until content is
ready.

### 7. Director detail has a broken image (Andrew De Zen)

One `<img>` on `/directors-roster/andrew-de-zen` has `src=` set to the page URL
itself — classic Webflow tell for a CMS image binding where the field is empty.
Likely the director portrait or a fallback image field. Audit the other
directors too before launch.

### 8. Grid tile posters don't render on mobile

On Music Videos, Film & TV, and Director detail every tile `<video>` sits at
`readyState=0` on mobile (webkit iPhone 14 Pro). Visually this makes the grid a
column of mostly blank dark 393×221 blocks with only the titles readable.

Two likely causes to investigate:
- Tiles have no `poster` attribute, so with `preload="none"` there's nothing to
  paint until the user taps.
- On mobile the controller should probably intersection-observer-autoplay the
  first N tiles, or at least set a poster frame from the video source.

This is the biggest "looks broken" issue on mobile that the user hasn't yet
raised — worth addressing alongside the other mobile refinements before client
review.

### 9. Filter dropdown on Music Videos / Film & TV has empty director entries

`.director-name-link.w-dyn-bind-empty` appears 7× on Music Videos and 2× on
Film & TV — these are Finsweet filter dropdown options bound to Directors whose
`slug` or `name` field is empty/null. Dropdown lists hollow rows that go
nowhere. Either filter the Collection List on the dropdown to
`is-a-director = true AND slug IS NOT empty`, or clean the offending Director
records.

## Cosmetic / acceptable

### Filter pills on Talent are `<a href="#">`

`ALL`, `ROGUE`, `REBEL`, `RESET` are anchors with `#`. They're wired with
`data-rogue-talent-filter` and work correctly, but semantically should be
`<button>`s or at minimum use `role="button"` + `preventDefault`. Low priority
— noted for a11y pass.

### Lightbox prev/next/close on showreel page are `<a href="#">`

Expected — they're driven by data-attributes, not href. Same a11y note.

### iOS Safari internal console messages on showreel detail

4 `Button failed to load, iconName = pip-placard / airplay-placard /
invalid-placard` messages. These are native video control chrome; emitted by
WebKit, not our code. Safe to ignore.

### Google Analytics requests ERR_ABORTED

Appears on every page in headless browsing but should work in real browsers
without privacy extensions. Worth a spot-check in real Safari / Chrome before
launch.

### `/sitemap.xml` returns 404 on staging

Webflow generates the sitemap on the custom domain, not the `.webflow.io`
staging subdomain. Not an issue — it'll resolve once the site is on
`roguefilms.co.uk`. Just confirm at cutover.

## How to regenerate

```bash
rm -rf test-results/qa-pass
npx playwright test code/tests/qa-pass.spec.js \
  --project=chromium --project=mobile-safari --reporter=list
```

Output lands in `test-results/qa-pass/<project>/<page>-{findings.json,full.png}`
plus a flat `summary.jsonl` at the root. JSONs include the full console error
stream, every failed network request, every bad link, broken image, grid
computed columns, and video element state per page.
